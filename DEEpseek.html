About Us

Welcome to the Department of Social Work. We are dedicated to advancing social justice and improving the well-being of individuals, families, and communities through education, research, and service.

Programs

We offer a variety of programs designed to prepare students for careers in social work. Our programs include:

  • Bachelor of Social Science (BSS)
  • Master of Social science (MSS)
  • Ph.D. in Social Work

Faculty

Our faculty members are experienced professionals and researchers in the field of social work. They are committed to providing high-quality education and mentorship to our students.

Events

Stay updated with our latest events, workshops, and seminars. Join us to learn more about social work practices and network with professionals in the field.
